Chaetomium ssp. and Stachybotrys spp. identification

Posted by moldyman1 on December 18, 2015

I was called to do an inspection after two offices had been repeatedly water damaged due to a roof leak.  Suspected mold growth was found behind the vinyl base cove.  I used clear transparent tape and clear fingernail polish on a microscope slide to prepare the specimens.   Identification to species after digital magnification on an S-5 Samsung phone was made with little difficulty.
