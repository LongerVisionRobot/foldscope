Who all joined us at the @DBTIndia #Foldscope workshop in Delhi

Posted by Manu Prakash on December 21, 2015

I am just starting to document the phenomenal experience of the series of #Foldscope workshop in Delhi, Guwahati and Kaziranga. As a means for people to cherish connections they made at the workshop – I am just documenting folks and organizations that joined us at Delhi. The location was Venky College in Delhi University. But, thanks to the financial support from @DBTIndia; we were able to engage and bring together participants from all around the country. Here is a short list of organizations that participated. If you joined us in Delhi and you are not listed here; please drop me a message/comment below. The goal of this post is for all of you to make connections with each other; so you all can connect and push forward the mission of “curiosity driven explorations” in rural and urban environment around the country.

keep exploring
