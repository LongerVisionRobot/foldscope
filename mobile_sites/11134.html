My first foldscope images!

Posted by rogelio on December 23, 2015

Last night I finally had some time to sit down and assemble the Foldscope I got few days ago at ASCB in San Diego. It was great to put it together and more impresive the things I saw! The first thing it ocurred to me to look at were some flowers that my grandma got few days ago in her birthday, so I took some samples from them (pistils and pollen) and snap a couple of pictures. Take a look! I have followed several posts in this site and it’s great to start now my own. I am really excited about using my foldscope!
