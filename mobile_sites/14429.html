How to make 100 foldscope slides for less than $1

Posted by Sri Vidya on March 31, 2016

A few days ago, my family sat together and made paper slides because we almost ran out of the slides provided. This is how we did it…
What you need:

  1. A white, thick poster paper (you can get 2 for a dollar at the store)
  2. A pair of scissors, or an Xacto knife
  3. Some time with yourself or your family 🙂

Here are the directions:
1. First, Take a “model” slide, or one of the slide provided with the set you got with the foldscope
2. Next, Trace the outsides of the slide “model”
3. Then, cut out your traced slides
4. Afterwards, label your “model” slide as in model slide,  Do not use

5.  After you have chosen your sample, you can put scotch tape over your sample as shown in the foldscope video

Enjoy!!!
