Week 1!

Posted by kbeatty on February 15, 2015

I sent my Foldscopes home on Thursday of last week with some directions, three slides I created, the magnets for the Foldscope and cell phone camera, and four blank slides made out of card stock. Since I have 120 students, I decided that my students could borrow them for a week at a time. By that Thursday night, I already had one student send me an e-mail to show me what he explored! One boy even asked for more blank slides so he could create more slides at home! Then, more pictures came in over the weekend. Here are a few pictures so far! I love that these students are getting excited about the world around them and I love seeing what they wanted to explore!

I will add more as the students send them to me.

All of these pictures are with the low mag lens.
