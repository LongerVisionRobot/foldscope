    For others to contribute and make suggestions for you; Could you please do the following:
    1) take a picture of the plant where you found it; and add the same above. Often insects and plants have very specific associations. Also; sometimes insects living on plant leaves leave a morphological change (such as galls) – it might be really worth looking.

    3) it’s possible it’s a kind of a nematode – I see something long in the egg case. A large number of nematode live as parasites on plants; it would be fascinating if you found one for money plant. Do take a look here:
