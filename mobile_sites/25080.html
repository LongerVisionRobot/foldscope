Moss suspended in water #BIO 60

Posted by nekogene on May 12, 2017

Hello hello!

This time I collected some moss on the ground and trees in a small water bottle and let it sit for about a day. I was hoping to see some tardigrades in it but I wasn’t expecting to.

And I did not see tardigrades.

 

However, I got a couple nice moss pictures and a small organism that whizzed around. I would like to see what kinds of things will float in the bottle after a couple of days. Also, my hunt for tardigrades will go on (because they’re very very cute).
