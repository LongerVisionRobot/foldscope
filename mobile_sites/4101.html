Sea-Cucumber

Posted by seacucumber on April 8, 2015

Before                                                                       After

We tested the fold scopes durability and how well it takes pictures before and after. We looked at a spicule of sea-cucumber under the slide.

This is the image before we tested the durability. To test it, multiple people stood and stomped on the foldscope, and then it was dropped.

This is the foldscope after the test. The image is slightly blurrier, and the lens opening seemed smaller, but it still works quite well. The foldscope has excellent durability and can withstand a lot.
