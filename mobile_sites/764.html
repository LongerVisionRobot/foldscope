Cheek cell-spread movies

Posted by laksiyer on January 6, 2015

Cheek cells stained with Methylene blue as in the previous post. The staining wasnt uniform at all and you can see precipitation in places. The non-stained cells provide gorgeous views of their details when in focus. Perhaps it is wiser to make videos and choose your best frames for nice pictures.

 

 

Below is a second video containing cheek cells processed as previously but stained with Janus Green. I was hoping to get some mitochondria stained, but failed. However, Janus Green also stains nuclei very well. This video has a steadier hand and is longer.
