curiosity catalyst

Posted by fabrikate on September 28, 2015

^ 1 blade of grass // $1 view // 1 million questions

what does a dragonfly wing look like up close?

does maple syrup sparkle when magnified?

what’s going on at the root of an eyelash?

what’s in a sneeze?

do you know the answer to these questions?

me neither.

not often does one meet the sort of tool that can change her perspective (quite literally) in an instant. i became with friends with foldscope 29 hours ago. as a result, a cascade of new questions has come rippling into my imagination, uninvited but welcomed. these questions are weird and unordinary and seemingly unrelated to each other. i am thinking about physical matter that i did not even realize i was curious about.

foldscope is a curiosity catalyst. let’s hope i don’t kill the cat.

my name is kate. i study people. i am a professional question-asker and an unprofessional explorer.

nice to meet you, microcosmos.
