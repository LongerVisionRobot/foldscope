Using a Foldscope at the NJ Science Convention

Posted by jbazler on October 15, 2015

On October 14, 2015 Dr. Judith Bazler and Carleigh Engstrom shared the Foldscope technology with other teachers and students at the New Jersey Science Convention (NJSC). The presentation involved hands on activities where attendees had the opportunity to use a Foldscope, create sand sample slides, look at pre-prepared slides, and take pictures using their smartphones. All attendees were extremely impressed with the Foldscope, and were curious about when they would be able to purchase one.

All photos were taken on October 14, 2015 at theNew Jersey Science Teachers Conference “50 cent Microscope, Foldscope” sectional workshop given by Dr. Judith Bazler and Carleigh Engstrom.
